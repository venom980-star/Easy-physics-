1. What is Kinematics?

Kinematics = Study of motion without cause.

  • Only deals with position, velocity, acceleration.
  • No force, no mass involved.

2. Types of Motion

  • 1D Motion ‚Äì Straight line (e.g. train on track)
  • 2D Motion ‚Äì Plane motion (e.g. projectile)
  • 3D Motion ‚Äì Full space (e.g. flying bird)

3. Basic Quantities

  • Distance: Total path (scalar)
  • Displacement: Shortest path from A to B (vector)
  • Speed: Rate of distance
  • Velocity: Rate of displacement
  • Acceleration: Rate of velocity change
